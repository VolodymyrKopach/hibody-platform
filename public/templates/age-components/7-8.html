🎯 Topic: Present Simple Tense

Present Simple is a tense that we use to describe usual actions, facts, and regular events. It shows what happens always or often.

Formula: Subject + Verb (+ s/es for he/she/it)

📚
Grammar Table
alwaysusuallyoftensometimesnever
💡 Examples:
I play football every day. - I play football every day.
She reads books in the evening. - She reads books in the evening.
They go to school by bus. - They go to school by bus.
