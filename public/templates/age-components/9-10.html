🎯 Topic: Fractions and Percentages

📋 Theoretical Material

A fraction is a number that represents a part of a whole.

A percentage is a fraction with a denominator of 100.

To convert a fraction to a percentage, multiply by 100.

🔍 Key Concepts:

  • 1/2 = 50%
  • 1/4 = 25%
  • 3/4 = 75%

📝 Formulas:

  • Fraction = numerator/denominator
  • Percentage = (fraction × 100)%
